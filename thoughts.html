Our Game Thoughts

Exciting!

We've thought a lot about games.

The Blue Zebra Team has gotten interested in idle games for a few compelling reasons.
Idle games currently comprise the top four games on Kongregate, one of the most used online game distributing services. Reddit source

We were inspired by Sandcastle Builder, a truly innovative and surprising game which was meant to be completely filled with inside jokes for people who had read a forum about a particular xkcd comic (#1190: Time)

We see a gap in the market and a real place for a game with better user interface and consistent creativity.

We are fascinated with the reward hierarchy utilized in idle games. Particularly interesting to us is the “soft reset” which is a choice presented to a player to zero out what he or she has accumulated in various categories of game assets and receive a high-level asset in exchange. The new asset increases the speed of low-level asset production.
